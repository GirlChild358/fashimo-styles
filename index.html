Fashimo Styles Studios is a proudly owned fashion brand dedicated to embracing culture and uniqueness, all in a stylish flair. Specializing in selling high-quality fabric and materials, custom clothing tailored to individual preferences and beautifully designed ready-made clothing, our business caters to all who values both authenticity and quality. Our clothing is inspired by modern trends mixed with African heritage, if preferred, all the while blending vibrant and bold prints, embroidery, complex stitching, quality textures and tailoring to create a statement for every event. Fashimo Styles Studios is the number one shop that offers clothing that allows you to express yourself authentically, whether it is for a wedding, corporate event, or a casual event. Our team of skilled designers work personally with each client to guarantee satisfaction and so the design reflects their personality. Each piece is carefully crafted with care and attention to detail. We are dedicated to sourcing high-quality fabrics locally, which allows us to assist support other small businesses while we also grow. At Fashimo Styles Studios, fashion is more than just clothing, it is a lifestyle and a way to radiate with confidence without saying a word. Our goal is to empower our customers to feel confident, beautiful, handsome, and comfortable in their clothing.

Our dream is for Fashimo Styles Studios to be a household name in South Africa and beyond, not just for fashion but for community, creativity and self-confidence. We see a future where our designs are worn with pride all over the world, where fashion sparks conversations and tells stories. We want to grow our clothing to include more styles, sizes and accessories for everyone, so everyone can find something that expresses their personal style. We will also work with other local craftspeople, seamstresses and artists to give up and coming talent a platform to grow in the fashion industry. Our goal for the near future is to host seasonal fashion shows and cultural events that showcase our designs and the powerful voices and lived experiences that inspire each piece. Through fashion these events will create meaningful connections and encourage others to be themselves. When you shop with us you are not just buying high quality, handmade clothing, you are supporting a proudly African brand that represents empowerment, identity, creativity and self-pride.
